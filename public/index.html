Product

Transcribing , Summarization, and Automating the Creation of Notes

Text summarization is the process of generating short, fluent, and most importantly accurate summary of a respectively longer text document. The main idea behind automatic text summarization is to be able to find a short subset of the most essential information from the entire set and present it in a human-readable format. As online textual data grows, automatic text summarization is helpful because more useful information can be read in a short time.

Design

Keeping it Simple. Our design goal with this product is to provide an easy interface so users can drive value without having to learn yet another piece of technology. The name of this company and application was very deliberate to drive one of our core compentency around addressing complicated solutions with "simple" tools. Notes Simple.

Technology

Advanced algorythms, machine learning, and a modernized technology stack powers the platform to produce accurate summrizations of large text files.

Self Service

Notes Simple empowers users to find required solutions and complete tasks independently. And with an integrated Google authentication makes tasks like signing up for the app even easier.

Features

Powering Notes Simple

Here are the four essential components

Summarization
Quickly getting the main points of a long article, document, or presentation by sifting through large amounts of text to find relevant information and creating more manageable version.
Voice to Text
Record a long presentation or lecture, upload the audio file, and it will be converted to text which can be used to automaticly create notes.
Highlights
Add keywords when creating notes to highlight content like formuals, images, or other information and the system will provide examples.
Downloads
All the transcribed full text files, generated notes, and highlights can be downloaded directly or emailed.
